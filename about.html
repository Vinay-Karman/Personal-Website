Who I Am

Hello! I'm Vinay Karman, a student currently pursuing a BTech in Mathematics and Computing. Originally from the beautiful town of Shillai in Sirmaur, Himachal Pradesh, I am passionate about blending technology with creativity.

I grew up surrounded by the epic mountains of Himachal Pradesh. School started for me at R.S.V.N School in Shillai. Then it was on to Carmel Convent School and Career Academy School, both in Nahan. It was an awesome journey, tons of learning that really helped me develop my thinking & analytical skills.
